Privacy Policy

Information about data collection, use and protection

1. Introduction

Your privacy and data security are our priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information on our website igorfloriani.com.

We comply with international data protection standards and respect the privacy rights of all our users.

2. Data Collection

We collect data transparently and only what is necessary to provide our services:

2.1 Data You Provide to Us

  • Contact Form: Name, email and message when you submit a project proposal
  • Direct Communication: Via WhatsApp, LinkedIn or professional email
  • Language Preferences: Your language choice (Portuguese or English) is saved locally in your browser

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Only after your approval in the cookie banner, we collect
    • Pages visited
    • Time on site
    • Button clicks (CTAs)
    • Device information (browser type, operating system)
    • Approximate location (country/city level)
  • Functional Cookies: Your analytics consent is saved in a cookie so we don't ask again
  • Server Data: IP address, access timestamp, referrer (source page)

3. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to your messages and project proposals
  • Improving the site experience (navigation analysis)
  • Understanding which sections are most visited and which CTAs convert best
  • Optimizing performance and identifying technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations (record retention)

4. Consent and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Analytics: You choose "Accept" or "Decline" in the cookie banner when accessing the site
  • Language: Your preference is saved in your browser's localStorage, not on a server
  • Data Removal: If you submitted data via form and want it removed, contact us
  • Consent Revocation: You can change your analytics decision by clearing your browser's localStorage

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data is NOT sold, rented or shared with third parties, except:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous navigation data is sent to Google Analytics (according to your approval)
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or court order

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS: All communication with the site is encrypted
  • Content Security Policy (CSP): Protection against XSS attacks and code injection
  • Security Headers: X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy
  • No Sensitive Storage: Personal data is not stored on our servers (only processed via GA4)

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies only for:

  • Analytics Consent: A cookie saves your choice to accept or decline analysis
  • Google Analytics: GA4 uses cookies to track your session (only if you consent)
  • Language Preference: localStorage (not cookie) to remember your preferred language

You can disable cookies at any time in your browser settings. This may affect the experience, but the site will remain accessible.

8. Data Retention

Data is kept for as long as necessary:

  • Contact Form: Up to 90 days after response (or per your request)
  • Google Analytics: Default retention of 14 months
  • Consent Cookies: 1 year (you can remove at any time)

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Know what data we have about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in structured format
  • Objection: Object to data processing for specific purposes

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the channels below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of the site implies acceptance of the changes.
